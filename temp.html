Enhancing Steel Production and Industry Development: A Strategic Vision

The Ministry of Steel aims to enhance steel production capacity, currently at 109 million tonnes, with an approximate production of 90 million tonnes, to support the growth of the steel industry. It ensures the quarterly monitoring of capital expenditure and modernization or expansion programs of public sector enterprises (PSEs) while facilitating the adequate availability of raw materials from both domestic and overseas sources. The Ministry actively promotes and facilitates research and development in the steel sector, monitors key performance parameters of iron and steel production, and oversees mergers, acquisitions, and joint ventures involving PSEs under its jurisdiction. Additionally, it strives to enhance customer satisfaction by ensuring prompt responses and addressing customer concerns in line with the service delivery standards outlined in its citizens' charter.
